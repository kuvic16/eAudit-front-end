TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN

Statement of understanding related to membership and association by BRAC staff in an external organisation.

Dear [Name of the programme/function/unit head],

I acknowledge and agree to the following responsibilities related to my membership/association in [Name of the organisation] as [name of the role/capacity of engagement]. Any violation will entitle BRAC to ask for discontinuation of my association with the organisation and take organisational measures, as relevant.

  1. I have read and will comply with the BRAC Safeguarding policies in all aspects of the membership and representation

  2. I have read and will comply with the BRAC Communications Guidelines in all aspects of the membership and representation

  3. I have read and will comply with the BRAC Conflict of Interest and Disclosure policies in all aspects of the membership and representation

  4. I will maintain confidentiality of organisational information

  5. I will not jeopardise organisational reputation through any action or statement expressed in public or private forums as part of my role with the organisation

  6. I will share lessons learned from my engagements with BRAC (as relevant) to facilitate knowledge transfer and skills development of team members

  7. I will ensure handover of my responsibilities and relationship to the incumbent if I move into a new role within BRAC or discontinue my service with BRAC which will entail that I can no longer serve in the organisation in my current capacity

  8. I will not receive any compensation/gift/benefit in cash or kind without prior approval and disclosure
